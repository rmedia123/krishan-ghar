About Us

Krishna's Home for
Quality Indian Goods

Krishan Ghar — meaning "Krishna's Home" — is a wholesale importer and distributor bringing the finest Indian kitchen goods to California. We import directly from India and source domestically to provide Indian grocery stores with the authentic products their customers know and love.

Named after Lord Krishna, the Hindu deity symbolizing protection, love, and divine grace, our name reflects our commitment to being a trusted home for quality goods. Every product we carry connects Indian families in America to the traditions and comforts of home.
